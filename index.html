How to Complete a Quest

1
Open the app and tap your name tab at the top
2
Tap Quests at the bottom → tap Main Quests
3
When you finish a chore, tap today's box (marked with ◆) — it turns yellow ⏳ (waiting for parent approval)
4
Once Mom or Dad approves, it turns green ✓ and your points go up!
5
Tap Side Quests for bonus points — reading, going outside, helping others, and more
6
When you have enough points, go to Loot 🎁 and claim your reward!
💬 Messaging Mom & Dad
Tap the Mom or Dad button in the top right corner to send a message. Pick your name, type your message, and hit Send.

👑 Parent Guide — Townsend Family Quest Board

Everything you need to manage the app from your device

🔐 Parent Mode PIN

1234

Tap the Lock button (top right) and enter this PIN to access parent features. Change your PIN anytime inside Parent Mode → PIN tab. Changes sync instantly to all devices.

✅ Approving Chores

1
When a kid taps a chore, it shows ⏳ yellow — waiting for your approval
2
The Lock button shows a red badge with how many are waiting
3
Enter your PIN → go to Home tab → tap Approvals
4
Tap Approve ✓ to confirm and award points, or Deny ✕ to send it back for redo

✏️ Adding or Editing Chores

1
Enter Parent Mode → Home tab → tap Quest Editor
2
Pick a kid from the dropdown
3
Fill in the chore name, description, points, and pick an emoji icon — tap Add Quest
4
To edit an existing chore tap Edit — to remove it tap Del and confirm
5
All changes sync instantly to the kids' devices — no refresh needed

🎁 Rewards System

Zero Budget Rewards (earnable daily)

  • 1 chore = Snack or 30 min screen time
  • 2 chores = Pick movie or 30 min extra sleep
  • 3 chores = Extra Xbox session
  • 4 chores = Skip one chore next week
  • 5 chores = Friend over or sleepover

Paid Rewards (Budget Active)

  • 30% core chores = $5 Cash
  • 60% core chores = Amazon item ≤$10
  • Full Legend Week = Xbox / New Movie

Points system

  • Core chores = 1 pt each
  • 1 pt = 3 free points for zero budget rewards
  • Golden Rules = 0.5 pt each/day
  • Fruit/veg or no-mess = 0.5 pt/day
  • Side quests = 1–1.5 pts each

🗑️ Clearing & Managing

·
Activity Log — tap Clear in Parent Mode on the Home tab to wipe the log
·
Claimed Rewards — tap Clear All in Parent Mode on the Loot tab
·
Family Posts — tap 🗑️ Delete on any post in Parent Mode
·
Events — tap ✏️ Edit or 🗑️ Delete on any event in Parent Mode

🔄 Weekly Reset

1
Enter Parent Mode — a Reset Week button appears in the kid tabs at the top
2
Tap it at the start of each new week to clear all checkboxes and start fresh
3
Points and redeemed rewards are kept — only the weekly chore grid resets

📅 Calendar Events & 💌 Messaging

Adding Events

Enter Parent Mode → tap Events at the bottom → tap Add Event. Fill in the title, date, day, and who's going. Saves instantly for the whole family to see.

Family Messages

Any family member can tap the Mom or Dad button (top right) to send a message. You'll see a red badge when messages are waiting. Read them from the same buttons.
